# Webinar Operating Roadmap

Date created: 2026-03-24
Webinar date: 2026-05-13 at 2:00 PM ET
Primary goal: 20+ registrations
Current signal at time of writing: 40 page views, 7 registrations, 17.5% conversion
Strategizer-aligned as of: 2026-03-24

## Should we build a calendar?

Yes, but it should be lightweight.

Build:
- a weekly campaign calendar
- a daily execution checklist
- a simple outreach tracker

Do not build:
- a complex social media calendar
- a many-channel publishing plan that creates fake busyness
- a detailed plan that ignores the current bottleneck

The bottleneck right now is qualified traffic, not page conversion.

## Big Plan

The roadmap from now to the webinar is:

1. Increase qualified top-of-funnel traffic to the live registration page.
2. Convert warm attention into registrations through direct outreach and follow-up.
3. Keep one credible public narrative running so the experiment stays visible.
4. Turn the best moments into webinar proof points and presentation material.

## Campaign Phases

### Phase 1: Warm Distribution
Dates: 2026-03-24 to 2026-04-05

Objective:
- turn the live page and new promo post into a steady stream of qualified visits

Primary moves:
- direct LinkedIn DMs
- WhatsApp forwards
- comments and follow-ups under the live post
- outreach to warm communities and likely amplifiers

Success signal:
- page views increase faster than content production time
- registrations continue rising without page redesign

### Phase 2: Repeatable Outreach System
Dates: 2026-04-06 to 2026-04-19

Objective:
- make outreach repeatable instead of ad hoc

Primary moves:
- build segmented outreach lists
- create 2 to 3 repeatable invite variants
- track who was contacted, who replied, and who registered
- identify the best-performing audience promise

Success signal:
- outreach can run in batches without rewriting the whole message each time
- one or two audience angles clearly outperform the rest

### Phase 3: Amplification and Social Proof
Dates: 2026-04-20 to 2026-05-03

Objective:
- use credibility, momentum, and proof to improve conversion from attention to registration

Primary moves:
- publish selected proof points from the experiment
- highlight live metrics, working agents, and practical outcomes
- ask warm supporters to share to one relevant person or group
- refresh the page and post assets only if the traffic story changes

Success signal:
- more signups come from forwarding and social proof, not just first-touch discovery

### Phase 4: Final Push
Dates: 2026-05-04 to 2026-05-13

Objective:
- convert undecided people and late interest into registrations

Primary moves:
- follow up with all warm leads
- publish final reminder post
- send short RSVP-style reminders
- emphasize urgency, concrete outcomes, and who the session is for

Success signal:
- visible registration lift in the final 7 to 10 days

## Daily Plan

Use this operating loop each day:

1. Check metrics.
2. Identify the current bottleneck.
3. Choose one primary move for the day.
4. Run the minimum agent set needed.
5. Execute one public or manual action.
6. Log what happened and what changed.

Daily priorities by order:

1. registrations
2. qualified traffic
3. outreach backlog health
4. public proof and narrative
5. system cleanup
